Mapping Methodology

We are approaching 100 exemplar institutes based on analytics of peer-reviewed contributions to help identify opportunities for improving existing tools.

We are utilising the PubMed API to gain programmatic access to PubMed literature content. We have developed a script and defined multiple queries to identify 10000+ papers, written between January 2016 and September 2019 on relevant topics. To ensure GDPR compliance we are only storing the name of the institutes and relevant departments. By December, we will conclude a thematic analysis of the papers and a survey to categorise existing software by attributes e.g. condition types, health data type.
